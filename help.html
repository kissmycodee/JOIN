What is Join?

JOIN is a Kanban tool that gives small and medium sized teams a simple way to manage, prioritize and distribute their work.

In a simple application environment, new tasks can be created and distributed to existing or new team members.

Before getting started, check out our short guideline to have a small glimpse how to work with JOIN!

How to use it?

1. Summary: In this section you get an overview of pending tasks and the upcoming deadline. A perfect way to start your work.
2. Board: All tasks are listed in the specific current status. To set a new status you can easily move the tasks by drag & drop to another column. Furthermore you can edit the tasks by clicking on a single card.
3. Add Task: Create a task which will be visualized on the Board-Category.
3. Contacs: In this section you get an overview of existing contacts and have the possibility to edit and manage existing, and add new contacts.
